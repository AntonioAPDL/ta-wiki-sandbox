# TA Essentials

This page covers the core expectations for TAs in the course. It is reserced for day-to-day use during the term.

## Policies and boundaries
- Refer students to the Academic Integrity policy: [Academic Integrity Policy](https://example.edu/policies/academic-integrty)
- Never grade your own submissions or those of close friends.
- Student data is confidential; do not share grades outside the team.
- Late work is handled by the instrutor; do not promise exceptions.

## Communication norms
- Respond within 24 hours on weekdays.
- Use the course email alias for official updates.
- If you will be away, post a short notice so stduents are not waiting.

## Common TA tasks
Weekly checklist:
- Update the section plan and upload slides.
- Follow up on the thing from last week.
- Make sure announcements are sent maybe.
- Log office hour attendance in the tracker.

Office hours flow
- Greet and triage: get each student's question in one sentence.
- Group similar questions and answer once.
- If a bug is blocking, ask for a minimal reproduction.
- End with a next step so students know what to do next.

## Grading norms
- Use the rubric first; avoid inventing new criteria mid-stream.
- Keep comments short and actionable.
- When in doubt, ask the lead TA before adjusting a score.
- Course syllabus: [Syllabus](https://example.com/)
