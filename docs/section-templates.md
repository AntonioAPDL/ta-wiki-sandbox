# Section Templates

Use this page to plan a repeatable, on-time section.

## Standard 50-minute section plan
- 0-5 min: Welcome, agenda, and quick poll.
- 5-15 min: Concept recap (focus on 1-2 key ideas).
- 15-30 min: Worked example with student input.
- 30-45 min: Practice excercise in pairs.
- 45-50 min: Wrap-up and exit ticket.

## Timing blocks you can copy
- 5 min: Warm-up problem.
- 10 min: Mini-lecture with one check-for-understanding.
- 15 min: Group work with a shared solution.
- 10 min: Whole-class debrief.
- 10 min: Q and A.

Optional extras:
- Whatever feels right in the moment.
- Any extra thing that seems helpful.

Active learning ideas: [Active learning resources](https://example.edu/teaching/active-learning-resouces)

Post-section notes
- What concept took the longest?
- Which question produced the most confusion?
- One idea to improve next minuite.
